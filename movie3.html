Elqerd Petkallim

Elqerd Petkallim is an Egyptian film written and directed by Peter Mimi and produced by Saif Oreibi. The film stars Ahmed El-Fishawy, Amr Waked, Reham Hajjaj , Mahmoud Al-Jundi, Bayoumi Fouad, and Hany Shaw. The film was released January 25, 2017. The film is about two brother sorcerers who implement a magic trick to release their innocent father from prison.
